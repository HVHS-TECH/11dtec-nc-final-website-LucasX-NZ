Clubs Based in wellington

Clubs outside of wellington

  • Wellington Milsim Airsoft (WMA)

    • One of the largest and most established clubs in the Wellington region. WMA focuses strongly on milsim-style objective games, teamwork, and structured events.

  • Project Phoenix

    • A Airsoft group focused on training, tactics, squad movement, and military-style teamwork.

  • RuB Core

    • Long-running Airsoft club founded in 2004. Publicly describes itself as focused on hard, fast gameplay, fun, and strong club culture.

  • 232 Crew

    • 232 Crew is a airsoft club focused on milsim gameplay, teamwork, and mission-based games rather than fast casual matches.

  • Airsoft Auckland

    • Runs regular games, large events, and skirmishes for players of all skill levels. Focuses on active community play and varied game modes.

  • Canterbury Softair Group

    • Organises outdoor airsoft games, training, and club events in Christchurch. Welcomes beginners and experienced players.

  • Dunedin Airsoft Club

    • Runs regular local games and social events for southern players. Focuses on teamwork and casual competitive matches.

  • TAG Airsoft

    • Hosts tactical outdoor games and scenario-based matches in Canterbury. Known for organized gameplay and realistic field experiences.
