what is airsoft?

Airsoft is a recreational activity where participants use replica firearms that fire plastic pellets. It is often played in outdoor settings and can involve various game modes such as team-based scenarios, objective-based missions, and casual skirmishes.


Clubs

New Zealand may not have the largest airsoft community, but there are still a number of clubs across the country where players can connect, play, and share their passion for the sport.

Clubs outside of wellington

  • Wellington Milsim Airsoft (WMA)

    • WMA focuses strongly on milsim-style objective games, teamwork, and structured events.

  • Project Phoenix

    • An Airsoft group focused on training, tactics, squad movement, and military-style teamwork.

  • RuB Core

    • Focuses on hard, fast gameplay, fun, and strong club culture.

  • 232 Crew

    • Targets milsim gameplay, teamwork, and mission-based games rather than fast casual matches.

  • Airsoft Auckland

    • Runs regular games, large events, and skirmishes for players of all skill levels.

  • Canterbury Softair Group

    • Organises outdoor airsoft games, training, and club events in Christchurch.

  • Dunedin Airsoft Club

    • Runs regular local games and social events for southern players.

  • TAG Airsoft

    • Hosts tactical outdoor games and scenario-based matches in Canterbury.
